## Product brief

### Goal
Build a **resume website with chat capability** for **PM/PO applications**:
- Helps recruiters/hiring managers quickly understand fit
- Lets visitors either **chat** or **browse** experience/projects
- Converts relevant conversations into a **handoff** (email capture) for follow-up

### Primary user types
- **Hiring manager / recruiter**: wants fast signal and relevant examples
- **General visitor**: curious, wants to browse or learn more

### Core experience (2-screen model)
- **Home (first screen)**:
  - Full-width background photo
  - 1–2 sentence positioning statement (PM/PO oriented)
  - Chat entry point (single message box)
  - Link to “Browse my experience"
- **Response (split view)**:
  - Left: “Related experience” (auto-filtered)
  - Right: ongoing chat
